Candorhub

Art Critique Web Application

Candorhub was built using React/Redux. Promoting constructive and meaningful criticism, users are presented with preset questions regarding a randomly chosen piece of artwork. As they type, their critiques are analyzed by a sentimentality library. If a comment or word is deemed inappropriate, the user cannot submit their critique.
